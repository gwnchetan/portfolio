MY STORY

My name is Chandrbhan Sakre — most people call me Chetan. I'm a Full Stack Developer from Rajkot, Gujarat, freshly graduated with a BCA from RK University (2023–26).

My obsession with computers started at 15 and never stopped. Through college I went deep — web development, Android apps, cloud platforms, and cybersecurity. Security especially pulled me in. I'm someone who gets curious, goes deep, and keeps building.
